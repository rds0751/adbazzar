About Ashwani Shekhri

Ashwani Sekhri is an Indian Politician and a member of Indian National Congress. He is the member of Punjab Legislative Assembly (MLA) and represents Batala which is the largest city in the state of Punjab. He was born on 26th March 1959. Ashwani Sekhri was born and brought up in Gurdaspur, Batala. He is a law graduate from Punjab University, Chandigarh in the year 1981.
Devoted to Social service with special emphasis on promotion of Education and Educational Activities.

Background

Political career:
Sekhri first successfully contested for Punjab Legislative Assembly from Batala in 1985.
He was re-elected from Batala in 2002 and 2012. In 2002, he was appointed Minister of State for Tourism and Culture. In 2009, he was appointed spokesperson of Punjab Pradesh Congress Committee.
He was one of the 42 INC MLAs who submitted their resignation in protest of a decision of the Supreme Court of India ruling Punjab's termination of the Sutlej-Yamuna Link (SYL) water canal unconstitutional.

He was successfully elected to the Punjab Vidhan Sabha in the year 1985, 2002 and 2012. He always had a clear vision of developing slum areas and housing for poor people. This dream brought him here when he has worked more broadly for social welfares. His father Shri. Vishwamitter Sekhri proudly says, ‘’my son has fulfilled my dream to work for poor’’. On the other hand, his mother keeps on encouraging him and believes that one day he would definitely create a big difference in India. This ambition made him the Vice President of Punjab Pradesh Congress Committee, (Congress-I). He Devoted his all time and effort to social service and broadly emphasis on Educational Activities.

Earlier, he remained the chairman of Punjab – Small Industries and Export Corporation. Also remained a member of several Committees of Punjab Vidhan Sabha. He got married to Smt. Anuja Sekhri who is Director in Abhinav Cotspin Ltd. After his wedding, he was blessed with 2 sons. He was re-elected from Batala in 2002 and 2012. This was the time when he was appointed as the Minister of State of Tourism and Culture. In 2009, he was assigned as spokesperson of Punjab Pradesh Congress Committee.

MLA loves traveling and exploring different parts of the world. He traveled to several countries which include U.S.A, U.K, Canada, Australia, and Pakistan.
